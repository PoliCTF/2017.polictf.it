Who are we?

We are Tower of Hanoi. We are a team of [B.Sc|M.Sc.|Ph.D] students and alumni from Politecnico di Milano, with a lively interest in topics pertaining computer security, cryptography and general technical prowess. After taking part in a number of computer security contests, in 2012 we had a sudden enlightenment and thought about organizing one ourselves. It seems we survived, so we're organizing the third edition of the contest :)
